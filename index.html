About

I am a PhD researcher in Mechanical Engineering at Cleveland State University. My research focuses on scientific machine learning approaches for modeling engineering and physical systems, with particular emphasis on data-driven analysis and computational workflows.

I am especially interested in bridging physics-based modeling with modern machine learning techniques to enable interpretable, scalable, and robust scientific discovery in complex engineering systems.
