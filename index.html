About

I am a PhD researcher in Mechanical Engineering at Cleveland State University. My research focuses on scientific machine learning approaches for modeling and analyzing engineering and physical systems.

I work at the intersection of physics-based modeling and data-driven methods, with interests in computational modeling, simulation-based analysis, and interpretable machine learning for complex systems.
