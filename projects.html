Projects

This page presents selected academic and engineering projects focused on energy systems, thermo-fluid processes, and data-driven analysis. The projects span techno-economic modeling, system design, and experimental prototyping.

Techno-Economic Analysis of Rural Micro-Energy Systems

Energy Systems Modeling 路 Distributed Generation

Conducted a comprehensive techno-economic analysis of a rural micro-energy system, focusing on optimal sizing and cost modeling of distributed energy assets. The study evaluated system configurations based on performance, capital cost, operating expenses, and long-term sustainability metrics.

The analysis supported decision-making for community-scale energy planning and informed design trade-offs between reliability, affordability, and resource availability.

Design of a Pyrolytic System for Liquid Smoke Production

Thermochemical Conversion 路 Process Design

Designed a pyrolytic system for liquid smoke production, including the selection of process parameters, thermal operating conditions, and overall system layout. The project emphasized process efficiency, controllability, and product quality.

System design considerations included feedstock handling, temperature regulation, vapor condensation, and operational safety.

Advanced Biomass Cookstove and Ground-Source Heat Pump Analysis

Thermal Systems 路 Sustainable Heating

Developed a metallic Tier-3 biomass cookstove designed for both cooking and space heating applications. The design prioritized thermal efficiency, emissions reduction, and durability.

In parallel, performed analytical evaluation of a ground-source heat pump system to assess feasibility and performance for residential heating applications.

Experimental Energy and Mechanical System Prototypes

Prototyping 路 Experimental Systems

Designed and developed multiple experimental prototypes to explore alternative energy and mechanical systems, including:

  • Compressed air energy storage laboratory setup
  • Parabolic solar Stirling engine prototype
  • Pedal-operated pond water cleaning system

These prototypes supported hands-on experimentation, performance evaluation, and iterative design improvement.
