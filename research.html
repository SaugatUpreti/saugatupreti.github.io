Research

My research focuses on the application of scientific machine learning methods to model and analyze engineering and physical systems. I am particularly interested in integrating physics-based understanding with data-driven techniques to improve interpretability, scalability, and predictive performance in complex systems.

Current interests include computational modeling, simulation-based analysis, and the development of machine learning workflows for engineering applications.
