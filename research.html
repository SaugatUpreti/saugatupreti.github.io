Research

My research investigates the application of scientific machine learning methods to the modeling and analysis of engineering and physical systems. I focus on integrating physics-based understanding with data-driven approaches to improve interpretability and generalization.

Current interests include simulation-based analysis, computational modeling, and the development of machine learning workflows for complex engineering applications.
